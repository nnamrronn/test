About Us.

A driven salesman set out on an adventure that ultimately would influence the jewelry industry since 1971. He possesses a strong awareness of market demand. He and his team worked tirelessly to perfect the craft of creating silver and gold-filled jewelry. Through tenacity and a relentless focus on quality, our business prospered and became known as a reliable supplier for the jewelry industry ability His team adjusted to changing industry trends. This dedication, together with our wide selection of products enabled us to establish enduring bonds with many large manufacturers and wholesalers.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission Is to be one of the top suppliers in sterling silver and gold-filled parts and tubing with an emphasis on hoops earrings. We have been dedicated to this since 1971. We are leveraging cutting-edge 3D printing technology and applying lean manufacturing concepts. We work to satisfy the changing needs of our clients. While extending our reach to the entire continental USA and abroad, we strive to offer affordable prices.

Our Vision Is to be recognized as the go-to manufacturer of gold-filled and sterling silver jewelry. We envision leveraging our extensive experience and expertise to consistently deliver innovative designs, and quality products, that meet the demands of an ever-evolving marketplace. By expanding our distribution network throughout the continental USA and overseas, we aim to bring our exceptional jewelry to a global audience, enhancing the beauty and style of individuals worldwide.
